The BI choice depends on the region: Tableau dominates in the US, while Power BI leads in the CIS and Europe (after Tableau left Russia). Looker Studio (formerly Google Data Studio) is the free option for pet projects. I focus on the two main ones and cover the rest briefly.

## What's inside

- **Tableau** — a full course
- **Power BI** — a full course with DAX
- **Looker Studio** — a free alternative
- **Metabase / Superset** — open-source
