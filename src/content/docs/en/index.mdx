  </Card>
  <Card title="Refresh a topic" icon="magnifier">
    Use search (Cmd/Ctrl + K) or the sidebar. Every page is self-contained — read from anywhere.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Interview prep" icon="approve-check">
    Each section has a page with typical tasks. The [Career](/en/12-career/) section covers resumes and interviews end to end.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Find sources" icon="external">
    At the end of each page there are links to external materials to go deeper.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Aside type="note" title="A reference, not a course">
This isn't a step-by-step course but a reference: open any section and take what you need — pages are self-contained and don't require reading the previous ones. It covers what's expected of an analyst at junior/middle level.
</Aside>
